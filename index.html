The Problem

Rising CO₂ levels cause global warming and ocean acidification, threatening marine life. Desalination plants discharge massive amounts of brine, harming biodiversity and wasting valuable resources.

Our Solution

GreenBrine offers a revolutionary system that extracts CO₂ from seawater, produces clean hydrogen (H₂), and recovers magnesium from brine waste, promoting a circular economy.

  • Electrolysis: Converts brine to hydrogen (H₂) and sodium hydroxide (NaOH).
  • CO₂ Capture: NaOH reacts with CO₂, forming bicarbonates.
  • Magnesium Extraction: Recovers magnesium from treated brine.
